Model

CTF uses the same phase term as the Streamlit app:
γ(s) = 2π(-0.5 Δf λ s² + 0.25 Cs λ³ s⁴) - (φ + asin(Q))

Envelope terms for B-factor, convergence, chromatic aberration, energy spread, current spread, and specimen motion are included.
